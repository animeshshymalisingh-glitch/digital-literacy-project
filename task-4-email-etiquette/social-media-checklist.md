# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's

1. Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles updated with your latest
   projects, skills, and academic achievements.
2. Think carefully before posting — ask yourself if you would be
   comfortable with a future employer or professor seeing it.
3. Use privacy settings on personal platforms like Instagram to control
   who can see your content.
4. Engage positively and professionally in academic or technical
   communities online.
5. Give credit when sharing someone else's work, ideas, or content.

## ❌ Don'ts

1. Don't share personal information such as your phone number, home
   address, or Aadhaar details publicly on any platform.
2. Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or hateful content — it can
   have serious academic and legal consequences.
3. Don't spread unverified news or forward messages without checking
   the source first.
4. Don't accept friend or connection requests from people you do not
   know, especially on personal accounts.
5. Don't use social media to vent about college, professors, or
   classmates — even a "private" post can become public.
